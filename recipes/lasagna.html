Lasagna

The Perfect Lasagna!

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with my delicious lasagna recipe. This dish layers perfectly cooked pasta sheets with a rich, savory meat sauce made from ground beef, garlic, and a blend of Italian herbs. Creamy ricotta cheese and a generous sprinkle of mozzarella create a gooey, melted topping that brings everything together. Baked to golden perfection, this lasagna is not only satisfying but also great for leftovers, making it an ideal choice for family dinners or gatherings. Enjoy it paired with a fresh salad and garlic bread for a complete meal!
